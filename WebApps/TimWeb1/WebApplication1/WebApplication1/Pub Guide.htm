Tim Blewitt's Pub Guide

Location

Pub / Restaurant

Comments
Albrighton Horns Of Boningale Good range of ales with good food.
Belbroughton The Bell Quite a large pub with an intimate feel inside.  Good food.
Belbroughton The Talbot Very good food in a country village pub.
Belbroughton Queens Another good food pub in the village.
Birmingham The Prince Of Wales Oasis of real ale in the desert of central Birmingham.
Bobbington Blakelands High quality restaurant - may be slightly overpriced.
Bridgnorth The Lion O' Morfe Very friendly pub with good food and beer.
Brierley Hill The Vine Home of the Bathams brewery itself.  "Blessing of your heart, you brew good ale"!
Claverley The Boycott Arms Good Sunday lunches.
Claverley The Plough Good country pub in beautiful village.
Clent The Fountain Need to book weeks in advance.  Worth planning though - food is very good.
Clent The French Hen French food in a pub full of character.
Cookley The Cookley Arms Consistently excellent food - adventurous options e.g. kudu.
Cradley Heath The Waterfall One of the best real ale pubs in the Black Country.
Cutnall Green Chequers Inn Very good food - run by the England team chef.
Droitwich Chateau Impney Top quality restaurant, particularly Angelique's.  Expensive but best steak around.
Dudley Himley House Good food served well.
Dudley The Crooked House Great place to take visiting friends.  The whole pub tilts at a steep angle and you must watch the ball roll uphill!
Greensforge The Navigation Great location on the canal in the countryside on the edge of the Midlands.
Harvington The Dog Not cheap but very good food.
Heathton The Old Gate Can be busy and may have to wait even if pre-booked!  Good food though.
Kingswinford The Park Tavern Good local pub.  Bathams always good.
Kingswinford The Summerhill Very local pub!
Kinver The Whittington Plenty of nooks and crannies in this 14th century inn for a good value bite to eat.
Romsley The Swallows Nest Good food.
Shifnal The Countess Arms Excellent food and service
Shipley The Fox Very interesting menu with excellent food, service and ambience.
Stourbridge The Retreat Cosy but intimate restaurant for a romantic occasion.
Stourton The Fox Very good food and beer kept very well also.
Wolverhampton The Great Western Best real pub in Wolverhampton.
Woodsetton The Park Inn Home of Holden's beer.
